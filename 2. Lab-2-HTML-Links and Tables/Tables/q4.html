AUTOMATIC SUBTITLE GENERATOR BY SOUND
Video has become one of the most popular multimedia artefacts used on PCs and the Internet. In a majority of cases within a video, the sound holds an important place. From this statement, it appears essential to make the understanding of a sound video available for people with auditory problems as well as for people with gaps in the spoken language. The most natural way lies in the use of subtitles. However, manual subtitle creation is a long and boring activity and requires the presence of the user. Consequently, the study of automatic subtitle generation appears to be a valid subject of research.
